Welcome to Camp

Ewing Woods Youth Camp is a one-week Christian summer camp set among the trees, running each year for campers 11 years old (or entering 6th grade) through 12th grade. Dates and details below are placeholders — check back soon for this year's information.

Outdoor Adventure

Hiking, canoeing, campfires, and archery led by experienced counselors.

New Friends

Small cabin groups designed to help every camper feel at home.

Unforgettable Memories

Traditions, talent shows, and stories campers carry with them all year.

Faith-Centered

Daily devotions and worship woven through a week of adventure.
