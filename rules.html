Camp Rules

You probably read these before, but read them again, before signing

  1. Campers must be 11 yrs old or going into the 6th grade up to 12th grade.
  2. Swimming at the lake allowed only with the camp director or counselor of boys or girls present. Must be more than one adult present at all times.
  3. Hiking will be in groups of four or more people along with 1 or two staff members.
  4. Restrooms shall be cleaned once a day. Group cleanup by assignment daily.
  5. Keep property cleaned up of all trash. Put trash in proper receptacles.
  6. Make sure you clean up after yourself. This includes the dining area, the restrooms, and the crafts & workshops.
  7. No fighting, bullying or physical contact. Must respect personal space. If problems persist, you will be sent home.
  8. Meals will be eaten at allotted times only. Menu and hours of operation are posted.
  9. Teens with cars may not leave the property for any reason until camp ends.
  10. All vehicles need to drive safely and slowly while on camp property. Use caution always.
  11. No quads or ATVs. (Maintenance dept. may use their quad for work issues only.)
  12. Air horn will be used for severe weather and emergencies.
  13. If you damage any property while at camp or at off-site activities, you and your parents will be responsible for the cost of all damages.
  14. Campers must attend all scheduled activities. May be excused by the camp director only, with counselor notified.
  15. Campers are not allowed in cabins during the day, only when deemed necessary.
  16. No drugs, alcohol, or tobacco allowed on the premises.
  17. If you bring harm to any camper, camp staff member, youth committee member, or any adult volunteer in any way, you will automatically be sent home.
  18. If something gets broken, please report it to camp staff immediately.
We expect all campers to abide by all camp rules. If you break the rules, depending on the severity, you will be subject to being sent home. You will not be able to return to youth camp for the remainder of the week. No refunds will be issued.
