About Ewing Woods Youth Camp

Ewing Woods Youth Camp is a Christian summer camp that runs for one week each summer, bringing campers together for a week of outdoor adventure, teamwork, and fun.

Our Mission

Campers experience an intense encounter with God's word in the midst of extreme fun.

A Letter From Our Director

Dear campers/parents,

My name is Carter Covert, and I am honored to be the dean/director of Ewing Woods camp for 2026. It is my pleasure to be able to help facilitate a camp showing the youth that they are valued and cared for in the kingdom of God. Campers will get the opportunity to dive into the word of God, while having an amazing time. We will be swimming, canoeing, playing games, watching movies, partaking in service projects, eating food and enjoying worship and fellowship. This is a week of camp that your camper is not going to want to miss.

If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out.

— Carter Covert, Camp Director

Who Can Attend

Campers must be at least 11 years old, or entering 6th grade, through 12th grade (as of fall 2026).

Our Values

Community

Every camper is welcomed into a cabin group that feels like family.

Growth

Trying new activities builds confidence that lasts beyond camp.

Nature

A week unplugged, outdoors, and immersed in the woods.

Faith

Daily devotions and worship point every activity back to God.
